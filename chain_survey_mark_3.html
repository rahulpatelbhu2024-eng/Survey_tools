Area Calculation

Shoelace + Trapezoid methods
Shoelace (Gauss) formula — exact area of closed polygon:
A = ½ |Σ(xᵢ·yᵢ₊₁ − xᵢ₊₁·yᵢ)|

Trapezoidal rule — area between baseline and offset line:
A = Σ [(O_i + O_{i+1})/2 × (ch_{i+1} − ch_i)]
